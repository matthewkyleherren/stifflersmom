Behance Graphic Design

Sahana is a wellness brand that offers different types of products for self and soul care. From home fragrances to oils and drops.

Description

From home fragrances to oils and drops, with the propose of connecting better with your own body and give you a hinch of nature in your day to day life. The color palette and names are based on the product their properties.

The illustrations are deformed with the intention of representing sensations and not concrete concepts. The layouts and overall cleanness in the art direction is what gives Sahana a transformative sense.
